HOW TO PLAY

Goal: Control more territory than your opponent.

  • Black plays first, then alternate
  • Place stones on intersections
  • Stones stay unless captured
  • Surround enemy stones to capture
  • No liberties = captured
  • Both pass = game ends

Scoring: Territory + Captures. White gets 6.5 komi.
