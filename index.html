HOW TO PLAY

Objective: Control more territory than your opponent by the end of the game.

Rules:

  • Black plays first, then players alternate turns
  • Place stones on intersections (not in squares)
  • Stones remain on the board unless captured
  • Capture opponent stones by completely surrounding them
  • A group with no liberties (empty adjacent points) is captured
  • The game ends when both players pass consecutively

Scoring: Territory + Captured stones. The player with the higher score wins.

Tip: Try to build solid groups with two eyes to make them uncapturable!
